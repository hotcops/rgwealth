82% of Our Business Comes from ExxonMobil Employees

At Rhame & Gorrell Wealth Management, almost 82% of our business is related to ExxonMobil employees, retirees or those referred to us by ExxonMobil employees and retirees.

This successful and unique experience has allowed us to have a thorough understanding of the various options and choices available to the ExxonMobil employee at retirement.

As a result of our experience working primarily with ExxonMobil retirees, our firm has developed an understanding that allows for an effective and seamless transition of retirement assets to be repositioned in the most tax efficient manner possible.

If you would like to begin this process of planning for retirement, please give us a call at (832) 789-1100 or email us at service@rgwealth.com, or sign up for one of our Retirement Seminars.
