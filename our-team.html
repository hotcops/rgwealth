Our Team

We believe a firm is only as good as its team. We currently have 8 college degrees (3 graduated with honors), 2 Certified Financial Planners and 2 enrolled in CFP programs on our staff. Everyone works in-house, together, on your behalf. A large team is necessary to provide the service clients expect and deserve, while also providing the security of a succession plan. A team gives you the peace of mind that you and your heirs will always have someone to help with your needs.

Michael R. Gorrell, CFP速

Senior Wealth Manager

Mike grew up in Fort Wayne Indiana and graduated from Valparaiso University in 1971. Upon graduation, Mike served three years with the US Marine Corp and was released from active duty as a Captain in 1974. Mike then joined Merrill Lynch to start his career in the securities business.

Along with his 42 years of experience in the industry, Mike has also worked diligently to improve his education by completing the requirement for the Certified Financial Planner速 certification at the University of St. Thomas.

Mike has been an adjunct professor with both the University of Houston and Lone Star College Systems and taught classes on a wide range of retirement and investment planning topics. He has also taught and continues to teach on & off-site retirement workshops for the employees of many Fortune 500 companies, such as ExxonMobil, Chevron, Reliant Energy, and Champion Paper.

In his spare time, Mike enjoys spending time with family, including his wife, two daughters, three grandchildren and several rescue dogs.

Henry Pizzutello

Chief Investment Officer

Henry grew up in Westchester County, New York and has lived in Connecticut for the past 25 years. He graduated from Georgetown University in 1986 with a degree in Business Administration.

Mr. Pizzutello most recently served as Head of Alternative Investments for Private Capital Advisors in New York. Henry has had a 25-year career managing assets for hedge funds, institutions and family offices. Prior to Private Capital, he served as Chief Investment Officer for Centaur Performance Group, and was the recipient of Barclay's HedgeWeek's "Best Credit Fund" in 2009. He has managed over $1 billion in assets for Canadian Imperial Bank, Jefferies & Co., and Nomura Securities International and was responsible for all proprietary investment activities. Mr. Pizzutello also served as Managing Director and head trader for the Bass Family of Ft. Worth, Texas, managing their convertible bond arbitrage group.

Henry moved to The Woodlands from Stamford, Connecticut with his wife of 30 years. They have four children. In his leisure time, he enjoys reading, playing golf, guitar and traveling.
